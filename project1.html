Production: BFA Thesis

Overview

Production is a visual exploration examining the utility of (graphic) design. This project investigates the various principles of modern design creation.

My approach to this exploration manifests into a self reflection - how I've encountered these concepts throughout my own creative journey. The poster serves as an illustrative map, compiled with compositions that represent production principles. Through an accompanying 90 page publication, the poster is dissected, providing visual and informational context. These pieces collectively form my introspective reflection on design production
