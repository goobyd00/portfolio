→ Production: BFA Thesis

Overview


Production is a two-piece exploration of the utility of (graphic) design. This project investigates the various principles of modern design creation.

The poster serves as an illustrative map, compiled with compositions that represent production principles. Through an accompanying 90 page publication, the poster is dissected, providing visual and informational context.

Key Skills

  • Illustration
  • Iconography
  • Color theory/palettes
