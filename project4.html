→ Underground Kitchen: Branded Packaging

Overview


The Underground Kitchen (UGK) is a non-profit organization based in Richmond, VA, dedicated to providing high-quality food to the local community. Collaborating with UGK as our client, my team and I worked closely to deliver various UX & design solutions to the organization.

As the visual designer on the team, I was responsible for creating packaging designs for UGK’s daycare catering service. The project involved developing concept designs for their snack bar recipes, which would serve as references for future packaging efforts.

Key Skills


  • Packaging design
  • Branding
  • Market Research
  • Collaboration

Snack Bar Packaging




In discussing the project’s direction with the client, we identified three key objectives for this project:

  1. Develop a visual language that resonates with UGK's core demographic—children ages 11-18. The design should balance playfulness with a sense of refinement.
  2. Create packaging that is not only branded but also ready for potential storefront sales, aligning with UGK’s future commercial objectives.
  3. Emphasize UGK's central message of community and nutrition throughout the design.



UGK Stickers for Temporary Branded Packaging




As a temporary solution, UGK stickers were proposed to add branding to existing food packaging. This cost-effective approach can be implemented immediately, which offers a quick branding option while the full packaging manufacturing process is in development.
