→ Swop: UX Case Study

Overview


Swop is a product "reselling" app with sustainabilty at its core values. Unlike other reselling applications, Swop focuses on the concept of trading to complete buyer/seller transactions.

The local matching feature promotes sustainable, cost-free consumerism, while giving users the same satisfaction as traditional online shopping.

Research


Competetive Analysis:


Depop
  • Focus on apparel
  • Users have history of exploiting unconstrained pricing
  • Trading is possible, however it is solely facilitated by users
  • In-app payment method (Depop Pay) can be complicated and inconvenient

Facebook (Marketplace)
  • Wide range of product categories
  • An extension of the main Facebook social media app, UI features become complicated for non FB users
  • No in-app payment method available
  • Communication between users are mainly self-sufficient

ThredUp
  • Focus on apparel, donating, sustainabilty
  • Typical online storefront, not user-centric
  • Trading is not possible
  • Sophisticated in-app payment methods
  • Includes features that highlight sustainabilty metrics
